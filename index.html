Privacy Policy — Shadowleaf Studios and/or Prints Scheduler

Shadowleaf Prints · Effective 11 June 2026

Shadowleaf Studios and/or Shadowleaf Prints Scheduler is an internal content-scheduling tool built and used solely by Shadowleaf Prints, an independent art studio. It publishes the studio's own original artwork to the studio's own Pinterest account. It has no users other than the studio owner and is not offered to the public.

What the app does

The app prepares Pin drafts of our own artwork. The account owner reviews and explicitly approves each individual Pin before the app makes a publish call to the Pinterest API. There is no bulk or unattended posting.

Data we collect

None. The app has no end users, no accounts, no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking. It does not collect personal information from anyone.

Pinterest data

The app accesses only our own Pinterest account, with authorization granted through Pinterest's OAuth flow. Information returned by the Pinterest API is used in the moment and is not stored; the app calls the API fresh each time it needs information. Nothing obtained through the Pinterest API is shared with, sold to, or combined with data from any third party.

Credentials

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the studio owner's own machine and are never transmitted to any server we operate or to any third party.

Children

The app is not directed at, or available to, children under 13.
